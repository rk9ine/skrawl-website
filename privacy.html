Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06/09/2025

Last Updated: 17/10/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Skrawl, a creative drawing and multiplayer guessing game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information (Authenticated Users)

  • Email address (for authentication via Google OAuth or email OTP)
  • Username (chosen by you, can be changed once)
  • Profile avatar (selected from our avatar collection)
  • Account creation and last update timestamps

2.2 Drawing and Creative Content

  • Drawing data (stroke information, canvas dimensions, drawing duration)
  • Drawing titles and descriptions (provided by you)
  • Drawing thumbnails (automatically generated from your artwork)
  • Drawing metadata (creation date, type, engagement metrics)

2.3 Game and Social Activity

  • Game session data (room participation, scores, round results)
  • Player points and leaderboard statistics (drawing points, guessing points, rankings)
  • Social interactions (likes/dislikes on drawings, view counts)
  • Game analytics (session duration, completion rates, player counts)

2.4 Guest User Data

  • Temporary session identifiers (for game participation)
  • Generated usernames (temporary, not linked to personal identity)
  • Local preferences (stored only on your device)

2.5 Technical and Usage Data

  • Device preferences (theme settings, sound preferences, input mode)
  • App usage analytics (daily active users, session counts, feature usage)
  • Performance data (for app optimization and error tracking)
  • IP addresses (for anonymous view tracking and rate limiting)

2.6 Purchase and Payment Data

  • Purchase history (items bought, purchase dates, transaction IDs)
  • Platform information (whether purchase was made on iOS or Android)
  • Payment information (processed by Apple/Google - we don't store payment details)
  • Purchase entitlements (what premium content you have access to on each platform)
  • App store account linkage (Apple ID or Google account used for purchase)
  • Refund requests (handled through app stores, not directly by us)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Provide drawing tools and multiplayer gaming features
  • Maintain user accounts and authentication
  • Enable social features (public drawing feed, likes, leaderboards)
  • Synchronize data across your devices

3.2 Content Management

  • Store and display your drawings in the public feed
  • Generate thumbnails for drawing previews
  • Track engagement metrics (views, likes, dislikes)
  • Enable drawing playback and sharing features

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Ensure system security and prevent fraud

3.4 Purchase and Premium Content

  • Process and validate in-app purchases with Apple App Store and Google Play Store
  • Deliver premium content (skins, themes, features) based on platform-specific purchases
  • Maintain purchase history and entitlements for each platform (iOS and Android separately)
  • Restore purchases across your devices on the same platform
  • Prevent unauthorized access to premium content and cross-platform purchase exploitation
  • Validate that purchases are only accessible on the platform where they were made

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (Database and authentication provider)
  • Google OAuth (Authentication service for Google sign-in)
  • Expo/React Native (Mobile app development platform)
  • Apple App Store (Payment processing for iOS purchases)
  • Google Play Store (Payment processing for Android purchases)

4.2 Data Sharing Practices

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data for advertising purposes
  • Public drawings are visible to all app users by design
  • Anonymous analytics may be shared with service providers

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies in database
  • Regular security audits and updates

5.2 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active; deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Drawings: Retained until you delete them; deleted immediately when removed
  • Game data: Active game sessions cleaned up within 30 minutes; leaderboard data retained indefinitely
  • Guest data: Session data cleaned up when session ends (no permanent storage)
  • Purchase data: Purchase history retained indefinitely to maintain access to premium content; preserved when you delete your account; includes platform information (iOS/Android) to ensure purchases are only accessible on the correct platform
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized data retained for up to 2 years for service improvement

6. Your Rights and Controls

6.1 Access and Updates

  • View all data associated with your account
  • Update your username (once per account)
  • Change your avatar at any time
  • Modify theme and sound preferences

6.2 Deletion Rights

  • Delete individual drawings from your profile (immediate deletion)
  • Delete your account (permanently deletes all data including login credentials)
  • Purchases are linked to your App Store/Google Play account and can be restored on any new account using "Restore Purchases"

6.3 Data Request Process

To exercise your rights:

  • Access/Export: Contact us at skrawlapp@gmail.com with your request
  • Deletion: Use in-app account deletion or contact us
  • Response time: We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request

7. Guest Users

Guest users enjoy enhanced privacy:

  • No personal information required or stored permanently
  • Local preferences stored only on your device
  • Temporary session data automatically deleted
  • Full functionality without creating an account

8. Children's Privacy

Skrawl is designed to be family-friendly but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the app and provide 30 days notice for material changes.

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, contact us at skrawlapp@gmail.com

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe we have not handled your personal data appropriately.
